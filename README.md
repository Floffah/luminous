# Luminous

Luminous is a long-running science fiction worldbuilding project exploring what humanity would do in a post-scarcity futuristic sci-fi setting.

Set in humanity's first permanent off-world civilisation, Luminous imagines a future where advanced technology has solved many material needs, allowing society to focus on community, stewardship, creativity, and discovery.

What began as a research settlement on the distant world of Emergence eventually grew into a thriving civilisation built around the city of Haven. Over time, its ideas would expand beyond a single world, laying the foundations for The Luminous Ring: a future alliance of worlds united by shared principles.

## Philosophy

Luminous asks a simple question: *If humanity had the opportunity to start again, what kind of civilisation would it choose to build?*

Rather than focusing on dystopia or conflict, Luminous explores an optimistic future that still grapples with difficult questions: technological responsibility, migration, sovereignty, personhood, and humanity's place in a wider universe.

It's not utopian, however, as Luminous battles with several fundamental ethical and moral dilemmas.

## This Repository

In this repository is:
- A Typst version of the personal wiki I've been developing over the past years, which documents the history, people, places, institutions, and technologies of the Luminous setting
- A Godot & Blender project where I attempt to model the world in a low-poly Valheim/Hytale-style world

Whether you're interested in worldbuilding, speculative governance, architecture, or hopeful science fiction, welcome to Luminous.
